## Step-by-Step:

### On Mac:
1. Open **GitHub Desktop**
2. Click **"GitHub Desktop"** in the top menu bar (next to Apple logo)
3. Click **"Preferences..."** (or press `Cmd + ,`)
4. Click the **"Accounts"** tab (on the left sidebar)
5. You'll see your signed-in account
6. Click **"Sign Out"** button (usually at the bottom)
7. Confirm if asked

### On Windows:
1. Open **GitHub Desktop**
2. Click **"File"** in the top menu
3. Click **"Options"** (or press `Ctrl + ,`)
4. Click **"Accounts"** tab
5. Click **"Sign Out"** button
6. Confirm if asked
